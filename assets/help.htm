Getting Started

1. Integrate with Email

Install SPEC and enter an email address.


2. Generate Keys

If you wish to save your private key to an NFC device do so now, otherwise press the “skip” button.


3. SPEC Is Ready!

You can now send and receive information securely and privately! Tap the SPEC logo on the top left screen to view menu options.


Send A Message

1. Select Recipient

Simply choose the recipient of your private and secure message, picture, video or file. It’s as easy as tapping your screen.


2. Write Message & Attach Files

YOUR CONVERSATION IS PRIVATE! Go ahead, include secret and private information. No one can decrypt your message but your intended recipient. That goes for any pictures, videos or files you attach!

3. Choose Delivery Method

With SPEC you choose how and where your message is delivered. Quickly send secure information from your company or private email account; even post secure information directly to a social network. Regardless of how you send a message, only your intended recipient can view the information.


Receive A Message

1. Click To Decrypt (Read)

Click a .spec file and SPEC will automatically decrypt and display your message. There are no passwords or additional buttons. SPEC integrates with most email and social networks. Here we demonstrate SPEC’s Gmail integration. Decrypt QR messages by selecting “Decrypt” under Menu.

2. Read Your Message

Hit the reply button to quickly and securely respond to a message, picture or video. View any attached file by clicking the attachment located at the bottom of the screen. If the sender is not a contact, press your phone’s menu button to automatically add this person to your contacts.


3. View Pictures, Videos & More

Feel free to send and receive your private pictures, videos and files.
It’s fast, easy and secure!

Share & Add Contacts

1. Add Contact

To add a contact, click the .spec file or scan the QR code. No need to enter passwords or edit contact information. Once added, communicate directly with your contact; safely and securely. Received a secure message from someone who is not a contact? While viewing the message, press the phone’s menu button and tap “add”.

2. Share Contact Information

To receive secure and encrypted messages just share your contact card. From the profile screen, tap the share button located in the top right corner; highlighted by the red arrow. Alternatively, you can hold up the QR code displayed on the profile screen and have a SPEC user scan the QR code (contact card).
